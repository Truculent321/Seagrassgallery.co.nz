About the artist

Grant Stevenson

I think I was always supposed to be a painter. It’s not until I retired from a busy life organising major public events in Wellington that I started to take painting seriously. You will see two basic styles of acrylic painting in the way I work. Some paintings are rendered in a tight, geometric, exact way showing an understanding of objects and perspective, others are more loosely created and happen more quickly. I shall keep going at both, perhaps until the louder voice drowns out the other. Grant Stevenson QSM

About

Seagrass Gallery is located at 38 Moana Street Otaki Beach New Zealand.

It is open every Sunday from October through March, 10am to 4pm (essentially the warmer months) or by appointment.
