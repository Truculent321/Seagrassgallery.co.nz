About the Artist


I think I was always supposed to be a painter.

It's not until I retired from a busy life organizing

major public events in Wellington that I started to

take painting seriously.


You will see two basic styles of acrylic painting in

the way I work. Some paintings are rendered in a

tight, geometric, exact way showing an understanding

of objects and perspective, others are more

loosely created and happen more quickly.


I shall keep going at both, perhaps until the louder voice

drowns out the other.



Grant Stevenson QSM
